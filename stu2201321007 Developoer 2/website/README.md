**PawHelp** is a modern, community-driven web platform designed to connect **people who need help** with **individuals willing to offer assistance**, for both **human and animal-related situations**.

The platform supports requests such as:
- Emergency transport or short-term care
- Temporary fostering or assistance with dependents (people or pets)
- Daily help such as walking, errands, or check-ins

PawHelp focuses on making local help **accessible, safe, and trustworthy**, strengthening community connections while supporting both people and animals in need.
